练习2

判断题

  1. In an expert system, the knowledge base ( KB ) and the inference engine ( IE ) must be stored together.

  • [ ] 正确

  • [x] 错误

KB 和 IE 在逻辑上是分开的，在物理上也不必存放在一起。

  1. If a fact that makes up part of the premise of a rule is not the conclusion of some other rule, then that fact will not be askable.

  • [x] 正确

  • [ ] 错误

不是其他规则的结论，就不是可以推出的中间结论，则可以询问。

  1. Model-based systems provide the ability to handle a variety of problems, including those not anticipated by the designers.

  • [x] 正确

  • [ ] 错误

模型的状态千变万化，对应不同的问题，均可以通过反复的假设、测试、排除、确认等方法解决。

  1. Rule_based heuristic approaches are large and slow as compared with model-based systems.

  • [ ] 正确

  • [x] 错误

利用精炼的启发式规则可以快速地从初始事实推导出最终结论。

实用的模型通常含有较多的部件和复杂的联结关系，导致模型较大，在模型上的计算、推理较慢。

  1. In Case-Based Reasoning (CBR), more cases, more intelligent, the performance will improve continually with the increase of cases.

  • [ ] 正确

  • [x] 错误

当个例较少时，more cases, more intelligent.

随着个例的增多，冗余个例、非典型个例也增多，检索和确定合适个例的时间也越来越长，导致整体性能下降。

  1. Given the following rules in a “forward-chaining” expert system application:

R1： A ∧ not(B) ⇒ C (0.9)

R2： C ∨ D ⇒ E (0.75)

R3： F ⇒ A (0.6)

R4： G ⇒ D (0.8)

R5： H ∧ ( F ∨ G ) ⇒ E (0.9 )

Given the following facts (with certainty factor ):

CF(F)=0.9 CF(B)= –0.8 CF(G)=0.7 CF(H)=0 .8

Use the Stanford certainty factor algebra to calculate CF(E).

【注】由 CF(B)= –0.8 可得：CF(not(B))= 0.8

∵ CF(F)=0.9, R3: F ⇒ A (0.6)

∴ CF(A)=max{0, CF(F)}*CF(R3)=0.9 *0.6=0.54

∵ CF(G)=0.7, R4: G ⇒ D (0.8)

∴CF(D)=max{0, CF(G)}*CF(R4)=0.7 *0.8=0.56

∵ CF(B)=-0.8, R1: A ∧ not(B) ⇒ C (0.9)

∴ CF(A and not(B))=min{CF(A), CF(not(B))}=min{0.54, 0.8}=0.54

​ CF©=max{0, CF(A and not(B))}*CF(R1)= 0.54 *0.9=0.486

∵ R2: C ∨ D ⇒ E (0.75)

∴ CF(C or D)=max{CF©, CF(D)}=0.56

​ CF1(E)=max{0, CF(C or D)}*CF(R2)=0.56 *0.75=0.42

CF(F or G)=max{CF(F), CF(G)}=0.9

∵ CF(H)=0.8, R5: H ∧ ( F ∨ G ) ⇒ E (0.9 )

∴ CF(H and (F or G))=min{CF(H), CF(F or G)}=0.8

​ CF2(E)=max{0, CF(H and (F or G))}*CF(R5)=0.8 *0.9=0.72

CF12(E)=CF1(E)+CF2(E)-CF1(E)*CF2(E)=0.42+0.72-0.42 *0.72=0.8376

  1. Hypothetical（假想的）device diagnosis.
    Suppose Q represents the domain of our focus, containing four hypotheses:
    H1, H2, H3, or H4 .
    The task is to associate measures of belief with hypothesis sets within Q

要求：

（1）给出初始的计算列表（保留三位小数，以下相同）

（2）计算用于归一化的分母

（3）给出最终的m5

Q有2^4=16个子集

证据e1支持{H1, H2, H4}成立的概率为0.8，说明其他子集对应的概率为0

则m1{H1, H2, H4}=0.8

m1{Q}=1-0.8=0.2

原因同上

m2{H1, H2, H3}=0.6

m2{Q}=1-0.6=0.4

m1m2m3

m1{H1, H2, H4}=0.8m2{Q}=0.4m3{H1, H2, H4}=0.8*0.4=0.32
m1{Q}=0.2m2{Q}=0.4m3{Q}=0.2*0.4=0.08

m3中其他子集的概率为0

同理可得

m4{H4}=0.9

m4{Q}=1-0.9=0.1

m3有4项，m4有2项，两两组合共有8项

m3m4m5
m3{H1, H2}=0.48m4{H4}=0.9m5{}=0.48*0.9=0.432

m5出现了两次空集，将被清零，m5{}=0.432+0.108=0.540

练习

判断题

全都是对的

  1. In an artificial neuron, activation level is determined by the cumulative strength of its input signals where each input signal is scaled by the weight along that input line.

    在人工神经元中，激活级别由其输入信号的累积强度确定，其中每个输入信号均沿该输入线按权重缩放。

  2. In an artificial neuron, the threshold function computes the neuron’s final or output state by determining how far the neuron’s activation level is below or above some threshold value or bias.

    在人造神经元中，阈值函数通过确定神经元的激活水平低于或高于某个阈值或偏差多少来计算神经元的最终状态或输出状态。

  3. The “designer’s choices” for a ANN’s architecture constitute the inductive bias of the system.

    人工神经网络架构的“设计者选择”构成了系统的归纳偏差。

  4. Multilayer signal processing means that errors deep in the network can spread and evolve in complex and unanticipated ways through successive layers.

    多层信号处理意味着，网络深处的错误可以通过连续的层以复杂且出乎意料的方式传播和发展。

  5. Backpropagation provides an algorithm for apportioning blame and adjusting weights accordingly.

    反向传播提供了一种算法，用于分配责备并相应地调整权重。
